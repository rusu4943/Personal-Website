About Me

Welcome to my profile! I'm Ruitong Sun, a student with a profound interest in applying AI in healthcare. My enthusiasm is particularly strong in the areas of health data and explainable artificial intelligence (XAI). I am fascinated by the challenge of understanding and dynamically controlling the inner workings of neural networks. In my academic and research endeavors, I focus on leveraging AI to positively impact healthcare, aiming to improve diagnostics and patient care. A significant part of my work involves making AI algorithms more transparent and interpretable. I am committed to the development of AI systems that are advanced, understandable, and controllable. Thank you for visiting my page. I look forward to opportunities to collaborate on projects that align with my interests in health data, XAI, and the dynamic control of neural networks.
